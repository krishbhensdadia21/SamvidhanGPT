About

  • 📜 The Constitution of India is the supreme law of the land.
  • 🕊️ It ensures justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity.
  • 📅 Adopted on January 26, 1950, it replaced the Government of India Act 1935.
  • 👥 It establishes India as a sovereign, socialist, secular, and democratic republic.
  • 🔍 The Constitution is a living document that evolves with time.

History 📚

  • ⚖️ The drafting of the Constitution began in 1946 and took nearly 3 years.
  • 🗣️ Dr. B.R. Ambedkar is known as the Father of the Indian Constitution.
  • 🏛️ The Constituent Assembly was established to frame the Constitution.
  • 🌍 It was influenced by various global constitutions, including the US, UK, and Ireland.
  • 📖 The Constitution originally had 395 articles, now it has 448.

Facts 💡

  • 🎉 January 26 is celebrated as Republic Day in India.
  • 🔑 The Constitution provides fundamental rights to all citizens.
  • 🌈 It includes provisions for affirmative action for marginalized communities.
  • 📊 The Constitution can be amended to adapt to changing needs.
  • 🚀 It aims to create a welfare state through directive principles of state policy.
